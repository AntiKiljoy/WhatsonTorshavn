Nólsoy vienpoint

Location: Between Hoyvík and Tórshvan
Difficulty: Easy
Duration: 30 minutes to 1 hour..

As you traverse the Boðanesgøta walking route, consider veering off towards Nólsoy Viewpoint

The tranquility of the spot, allows you to appreciate the surroundings and natural sounds of the costal environment.

Take a moment to connect with the rhythmic sounds of the waves.
